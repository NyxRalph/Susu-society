Ghana Co-operative

Susu Collecters Association

The Ghana Co-operative Susu Collectors Association (GCSCA) aligns its goals with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The primary SDGs relevant to GCSCA include:

1. No Poverty (SDG 1): Enhance financial inclusion and provide accessible savings and credit facilities to marginalized populations, reducing poverty levels.

2. Decent Work and Economic Growth (SDG 8): Promote sustainable economic growth by supporting micro, small, and medium enterprises, and create decent employment opportunities.

2. SDG 5: Gender Equality - GCSCA promotes women's financial inclusion and empowerment.

4. SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure - GCSCA improves access to financial services, fostering innovation and infrastructure development.

5. SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities - GCSCA aims to reduce economic inequalities by providing financial services to underserved communities.
